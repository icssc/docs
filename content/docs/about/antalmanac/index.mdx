AntAlmanac is the course planning app for classes at UC Irvine. As of Feburary 2026, you can both
create quarterly schedules and plan your academic journey with our 4-year planner.

<Callout>
    "AntAlmanac Planner" (formerly known as PeterPortal) is now unified with Scheduler under a single application.
    To learn more about the merge and what it means for us moving forward, check out the [merge](/docs/about/antalmanac/merge) page.
</Callout>

## AntAlmanac Scheduler

"AntAlmanac Scheduler" (formerly known as "AntAlmanac") is a schedule planner website for classes at UC Irvine. Its core features include: 

- **_Search bar_** to easily find classes by department (e.g COMPSCI), course code (e.g. ICS 31), and keywords (e.g. artificial intelligence).
- **_Integrated calendar_** to preview class times.
- **_Quick links_** to professor reviews, prerequisites, grade distributions, and past enrollment data.
- **_Interactive map_** with markers for your class locations.

To learn more about AntAlmanac Scheduler, please see the [AntAlmanac Scheduler Contributor Docs](/docs/contributor/antalmanac)

## AntAlmanac Planner

"AntAlmanac Planner" (formerly known as "PeterPortal") allows you to create detailed 4-year academic plans. Its core features include:

- **_Major, Minor, and General Education Requirements_** to help you plan each quarter
- **_Search bar_** to find prerequisites, previous offerings, and reviews for courses and professors
- **_Importing 4 Year Plans_** from unofficial student transcripts and Zot4Plan.
- **_Prerequisite clearing_** for AP Exams and community college courses.
